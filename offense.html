The Quarterback

Alex Smith, though currently injured, is the greatest quarterback I have ever seen. Ever been made fun of? Told you were incapable? Told you couldn't ever do somthing? Be replaced by someone better? Have you ever had to be the scapegoat for everyone else's failures? Alex Smith has done all of this. He was the face of San Francisco despite being in a toxic and dysfunctional work environment. And he persevered more than any other NFL quarterback ever would.
